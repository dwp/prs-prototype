What happens next

We’ve received your application.

If your tenant gets Universal Credit, we’ll try to deal with your application before their next payment date.

Sometimes we cannot manage this. For example, if:

  • we got your application too close to your tenant’s next payment date
  • they dispute your application
  • other action is needed on their Universal Credit claim
  • they do not get Universal Credit

Do not make another application for the same tenant until we’ve told you the outcome of this application.
