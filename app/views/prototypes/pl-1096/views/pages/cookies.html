Cookies

Measuring website usage with Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect information about how you use ‘Apply for direct rent payments’. We do this to help make sure the service is meeting the needs of its users and to help make improvements.

Google Analytics stores information about:

  • the pages you visit
  • how long you spend on each page
  • how you got to the service
  • what you click on while you’re using the service

We do not collect or store your personal information, so this information cannot be used to identify who you are. For more information visit our privacy policy page.

We do not allow Google to use or share our analytics data.
