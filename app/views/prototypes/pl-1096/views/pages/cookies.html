Cookies

'Apply for direct rent payments’ puts small files (known as ‘cookiesʼ) onto your computer or other device.

We use cookies to:

  • remember the notifications youʼve seen so that we do not show them to you again
  • keep your information secure as you move through the service
  • measure how you use our service so that we can improve it for you

We do not use cookies to identify you personally. You’ll see a message on the site before we store a cookie on your computer.

Measuring website usage with Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect information about how you use ‘Apply for direct rent payments’. We do this to help make sure the service is meeting the needs of its users and to help make improvements.

Google Analytics stores information about:

  • the pages you visit on ‘Apply for direct rent payments’
  • how long you spend on each ‘Apply for direct rent payments’ page
  • how you got to the service
  • what you click on while you’re using the service

We do not collect or store your personal information (for example your name or address) so this information can’t be used to identify who you are.

We do not allow Google to use or share our analytics data.

If your cookies are switched off

You’ll see an error message when you try to use the service if your cookies are disabled.

You cannot complete the application for direct rent payments if cookies are switched off.

If your cookies are switched on

How to turn on cookies depends on the browser you’re using and how up to date it is.

Find out how to change your settings for the most popular browsers, including:

  • Internet Explorer
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Google Chrome
  • Safari, commonly used on iPads, iPhones and other Apple computers

Find out how to manage cookies, for other browsers.

If you do not want to accept our cookie policy

If you do not want to accept our cookie policy on your own computer or device you can:

  • use a computer or device belonging to someone else (with their permission)
  • use a computer or device at a public facility such as a library or internet cafe
